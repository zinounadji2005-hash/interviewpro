You are working on a production-ready web application that provides AI-powered CV optimization and mock job interviews.

Task:
Add all essential legal and platform policies required for a professional, trustworthy, and compliant SaaS product.

---

1. Required Policies to Create

You must create the following pages and link them clearly in the footer and during user registration:

A. Terms of Use (Terms & Conditions)
B. Privacy Policy
C. Acceptable Use Policy
D. Refund & Credit Policy
E. Disclaimer (AI Usage Disclaimer)

---

2. Terms of Use – Requirements

The Terms of Use must:
- Define the platform as an AI-powered career preparation tool
- Clarify that the service does NOT guarantee employment or interview success
- Specify user responsibilities (accurate information, personal CV only)
- Prohibit misuse (impersonation, uploading others’ CVs, abuse of credits)
- Define account suspension or termination conditions
- Clarify intellectual property ownership
- Include limitation of liability
- Specify governing law and dispute handling (generic, not jurisdiction-specific)

Tone:
Professional, clear, non-aggressive, user-friendly.

---

3. Privacy Policy – Requirements

The Privacy Policy must clearly explain:
- What data is collected (name, email, CV content, interview answers, voice recordings if applicable)
- Why data is collected (service delivery, improvement, security)
- How data is stored and protected
- That CVs and interview data are NOT shared with third parties
- How long data is retained
- User rights (access, correction, deletion)
- Use of cookies and analytics (if applicable)

Important:
- Emphasize data confidentiality and security
- Do NOT claim compliance with regulations unless implemented (e.g. GDPR)

---

4. Acceptable Use Policy

Define what users are NOT allowed to do, including:
- Uploading CVs that are not their own
- Creating multiple accounts to bypass credit limits
- Using the platform for illegal or deceptive purposes
- Attempting to reverse engineer or exploit the AI

State consequences clearly but professionally.

---

5. Refund & Credit Policy

Clearly explain:
- How credits are purchased
- What credits can be used for
- That credits are non-transferable
- Conditions under which refunds are allowed or not allowed
- Handling of failed payments or unused credits

Keep this section transparent and fair.

---

6. AI Disclaimer

Include a clear disclaimer stating:
- AI-generated feedback is advisory, not professional hiring advice
- Users remain responsible for final CV content and interview performance
- AI may produce imperfect or incomplete results

This disclaimer must appear:
- In the policies
- Subtly within the product (e.g. footer or dashboard info)

---

7. UI & Integration Requirements

- Create dedicated pages for each policy
- Ensure all policies are accessible before account creation
- Add a required checkbox:
  “I agree to the Terms of Use and Privacy Policy”
- Link policies in the footer on all pages

---

8. Constraints

- Do NOT copy content from other companies
- Do NOT include false legal claims
- Do NOT overpromise compliance
- Keep language understandable for non-lawyers

---

Goal:
Provide transparent, trustworthy, and legally responsible policies that protect both users and the platform while maintaining a professional SaaS standard.
