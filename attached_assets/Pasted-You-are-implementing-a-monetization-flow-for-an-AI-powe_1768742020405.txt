You are implementing a monetization flow for an AI-powered CV and interview platform.

Core Business Rule:
New users must receive enough free credits to:
- Upload and process their CV
- Complete their first interview session

However:
They must NOT be able to view the final results, feedback, or analysis unless they purchase additional credits.

---

1. Free Credit Allocation

- Upon successful user registration, automatically grant a limited amount of free credits.
- These free credits must be sufficient to:
  - Upload and analyze one CV
  - Complete one full interview session
- Free credits are for trial purposes only.

---

2. Usage Flow (Critical)

Allowed with free credits:
- CV upload
- CV analysis processing
- Interview questions and user answers
- Interview completion

Restricted without payment:
- Final CV improvement output
- Interview evaluation scores
- Detailed feedback and recommendations
- Model answers
- Improvement analysis

---

3. Results Paywall Logic

- After the interview is completed:
  - Process all results normally in the backend.
  - Do NOT display results to the user.
- Show a paywall screen stating clearly:
  “Your interview analysis is ready. Unlock your results by purchasing credits.”

- The user must understand that:
  - Results already exist
  - Payment unlocks access, not processing

---

4. Credit Deduction Rules

- Free credits are deducted normally for trial actions.
- Result-viewing features require paid credits.
- The system must distinguish between:
  - free_credits
  - paid_credits (or track credit source in transactions)

---

5. UX Principles

- Be transparent: no hidden conditions.
- Do not surprise the user earlier in the flow.
- Show clear value preview before paywall (blurred results, partial insights, or progress indicators).
- Use respectful, non-aggressive language.

---

6. Technical Constraints

- Supabase is the source of truth for credit balances and transactions.
- Paywall checks must be enforced server-side.
- Do NOT allow client-side bypass.
- Ensure idempotency and consistency.

---

7. Future Flexibility

- Allow business logic to adjust:
  - Free credit amount
  - Which features are locked
  - Paywall trigger points
without code rewrites.

---

Goal:
Allow users to fully experience the product’s core value, then require payment only at the moment results are revealed, maximizing conversion while preserving trust.
